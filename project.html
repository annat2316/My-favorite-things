Welcome to Fort Mifflin!

Welcome to Fort Mifflin! Fort Mifflin is a great place for you to go for Halloween! It is said to be haunted by ghosts! It also has a long military history. If you are looking for a spooky but cultural experience, Fort Mifflin is the place to go!

History

Fort Mifflin has been in Philadelphia since 1771. It is one of the only forts from the Revolutionary War still standing today. It is also the only fort in Philadelphia. It served during the Revolutionary War, The Civil War, WWI, and WWII. It was a Revolutionary War battlefield and a Civil War prison. During WWI and II, It served as a part of the US Navy Ammunition Depot.

Planning Your Visit

It is open to the public Wednesdays to Sundays from 10am to 4pm. It is open from March 1st to December 15. Admission fees apply.

Halloween at Fort Mifflin

Halloween is the best time to visit Fort Mifflin! They have lots of events going on! They have Open Investigation Evenings, Candlelight Ghost Tours, and A Mud Island Halloween. Go to their website for more information about these events!
